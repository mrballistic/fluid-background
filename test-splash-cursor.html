Splash Cursor Demo

Move your mouse around this page to see the beautiful fluid cursor effect in action. The effect creates smooth, colorful trails that follow your cursor movement.

How it works

This effect uses a high-performance particle system that creates fluid-like splashes at your cursor position. The particles have physics simulation including:

  • Velocity-based movement
  • Smooth color transitions
  • Natural fading over time
  • Fluid-like spreading behavior

Performance

The effect is optimized to run at 60fps while maintaining beautiful visuals. It uses efficient rendering techniques and smart particle management.

Try moving your mouse in different patterns - circles, figure-8s, quick movements, slow movements - and see how the fluid responds naturally to your input!
